1 BayesOpt: A Bayesian optimization library
2 =========================================
3 
4 BayesOpt is an efficient implementation of the Bayesian optimization
5 methodology for nonlinear optimization, experimental design and
6 hyperparameter tunning.
7 
8 Bayesian optimization uses a distribution over functions to build a
9 surrogate model of the unknown function for we are looking the
10 optimum, and then apply some active learning strategy to select the
11 query points that provides most potential interest or
12 improvement. Thus, it is a **sample efficient** method for nonlinear
13 optimization, design of experiments and simulations or bandits-like
14 problems. Currently, it is being used in many scientific and
15 industrial applications. In the literature it is also called
16 Sequential Kriging Optimization (SKO), Sequential Model-Based
17 Optimization (SMBO) or Efficient Global Optimization (EGO).
